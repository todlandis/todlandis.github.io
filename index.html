This is the support page for ScopePad!
ScopePad is beta software.

PRIVACY STATEMENT
ScopePad collects no data about you.


QUICK START 
Drag left/right, then up/down to see what happens.
Left/right rotates the map, up/down raises or lowers the map altitude.

Tap the gear icon to change display options.

Tap the magnifying glass icon to Search.
Search sets both alt and az given a star or celestial object id.
All NGC, IC, Messier, and Caldwell ids are supported.  You can also
search by common name, e.g. "Dumbell".
Stars can be identified by their name or Bayer designation.  Use
three letter abbreviations for greek letter and constellation.
Examples:  alp ori, bet uma, gam cas
